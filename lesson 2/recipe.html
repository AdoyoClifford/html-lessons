Yummy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, melted butter, and egg.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix.
  4. Heat a pan on medium heat.
  5. Pour small circles of batter onto the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook other side.
  7. Serve with syrup and enjoy!
