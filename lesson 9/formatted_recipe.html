Yummy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan to about 500C.
  2. Pour small circles of batter onto the pan.
  3. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook other side.
  4. Serve with syrup and enjoy!
